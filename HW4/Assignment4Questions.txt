1.What is the stack’s order of insertion and removal?

Last in first out

2.What operation inserts an element in a stack?

push()

3.What operation removes an element from the stack?

pop()

4.From what part of the stack do all operations occur?

the top of the stack

5.Is a stack a linear data structure?

Yes, because it follows the LIFO rule and can only be accessed from the top.
